Rental Requirements & Documentation

Understanding the requirements helps streamline your rental application process.

Required Documents

  • Emirates ID (for UAE residents)
  • Passport and Visa (for expatriates)
  • Salary Certificate or Employment Letter
  • Bank Statements (last 3 months)
  • Security Deposit (typically 5-10% of annual rent)
  • Real Estate Commission (typically 5%)

Payment Methods

  • Annual payment (most common)
  • Semi-annual payments (2 cheques)
  • Quarterly payments (4 cheques)
  • Monthly payments (12 cheques - limited options)
